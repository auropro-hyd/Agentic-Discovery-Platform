1.1 Domain Overview
Opella Europe's Order-to-Cash domain covers the end-to-end process from customer order receipt through to cash collection across European markets. The domain spans two primary order channels — retail EDI (high-volume, automated) and manual/traditional (lower volume, distributor-led) — and involves four core systems: SAP S/4HANA for transactional processing, SAP CRM for customer relationship and credit management, an Order Management System for order processing, and a 14-connection EDI network serving major retail and pharmacy chains.

1.6 System Inventory
SAP S/4HANA (ERP)
Core transactional system. Customer master records, order processing, AR posting. 340 active European customer records.
SAP CRM
Customer relationship and credit management. Credit limit management, payment terms, account manager assignments. 318 customer records.
Order Management System
Processes incoming orders from all channels. Interfaces with EDI network for automated retail orders and SAP ERP for fulfilment.
EDI Network (14 connections)
Automated order exchange with major retail and pharmacy chains. Partners include Carrefour, Boots, dm, Walmart, Leclerc, Lidl, Tesco, and others.
AR System
Accounts receivable management. Invoice tracking, payment matching, ageing analysis, credit limit utilisation reporting.
Warehouse Management
Manages pick, pack, and dispatch instructions from OMS. Interfaces with DC stock management and third-party logistics providers.
