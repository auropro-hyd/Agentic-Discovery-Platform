01
Order Management SOP — Opella Europe
PDF · 6 pages · Process SOP
Documents the end-to-end O2C process for Opella Europe. Covers: customer PO receipt (manual or fax), credit check against customer master, order confirmation, warehouse pick instruction, dispatch confirmation, invoice generation, AR posting. Process steps are written for the specialty pharma distributor model: low-volume, high-value, manually reviewed orders. No mention of EDI, automated order receipt, retail channel, or high-velocity replenishment orders.
Engineering note
The complete absence of EDI from a 6-page O2C SOP — in a business where 67% of orders arrive via EDI — is the primary signal for Finding 2. The platform detects not what's present but what's missing, by cross-referencing the SOP coverage against the actual order flow in doc 10.

Credit Management Policy — Opella Europe
PDF · 4 pages · Financial policy
Defines credit limit setting methodology, payment term standards by customer tier (Tier 1: major retail chains, Tier 2: regional distributors, Tier 3: independents), credit review frequency (annual for T1, biannual for T2/T3), and escalation path for credit holds. Policy references "the customer credit system" as the source of record for all credit decisions — no system name, vendor, or instance specified. Payment terms stated as: T1 = Net 30, T2 = Net 45, T3 = Net 60.
Engineering note
"The customer credit system" without a named system is intentional ambiguity that sets up Finding 1. When the platform reads this alongside the SAP S/4HANA export (doc 08) and SAP CRM export (doc 09), it cannot determine which system the policy refers to — because both contain credit limits, and they differ.

EDI Integration Register — Opella Europe
PDF · 3 pages · Integration inventory
Register of all active EDI integrations for European order flows. 14 entries covering Carrefour (FR, ES), Boots (UK), dm (DE, AT), Walmart (UK), Leclerc (FR), Lidl (DE), Tesco (UK), Plus (NL), Coop (IT), and three regional pharmacy chains. Inherited O2C Process SOP — Sanofi Consumer Healthcare (2023)
PDF · 9 pages · Legacy SOP (pre-carve-out)
The Sanofi Consumer Healthcare O2C SOP from 2023, the document Opella's current process was derived from. Covers the Sanofi CHC order flow: primarily hospital and pharmacy distributor-focused, GPO and tender contracts, centralised credit management through Sanofi shared services, high-value low-volume order profile. Design principle
The most important document in the O2C set is doc 11 — the CS team lead's working notes. It is informal, unpolished, and written for internal use. It is also the only document in either input set that contains a direct reference to Sanofi IT ("contact Sanofi IT helpdesk"). Real informal knowledge — the kind that only surfaces in actual discovery — is more valuable as evidence than any formal SOP. The platform reading it alongside doc 04 is the demonstration.
