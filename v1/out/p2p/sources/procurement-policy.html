PROCUREMENT POLICY — Acme Manufacturing (Generic synthetic doc for cross-domain test)

1. All purchase orders must be raised in the procurement system before goods are ordered.
2. Any purchase order above EUR 50,000 requires approval from the Category Manager AND a second
   approval from Finance before it is released to the supplier.
3. Purchase orders are matched against the goods receipt and the supplier invoice (three-way match)
   before payment is released.
4. Maverick spend — ordering without a purchase order — is not permitted under any circumstances.

This policy describes the controlled, approved procurement process.
