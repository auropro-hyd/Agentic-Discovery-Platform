## The one-sentence takeaway

No incumbent occupies our intersection: process mining needs event logs, APM needs a maintained
repository, LLM-knowledge tools retrieve but don't compute over data, the Big-4 do it with people,
and carve-out tooling tracks/moves but doesn't *discover*. **We read the messy documents they
discard, compute the contradictions and unowned processes they're blind to, and deliver a grounded,
client-ready report suite — fastest and most defensible at a carve-out / TSA-exit moment.**
