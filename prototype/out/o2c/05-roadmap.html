Transformation Roadmap

Sequenced across three horizons, aimed at a Control-first consolidation: one trusted view of credit and ownership before automation direction.

H1 — Close the highest-impact findings with information already in hand (0-6 months)

  • Single trusted view of customer credit, with controlled release opportunity — Brings 30675000 EUR of credit divergence under review and stops silent over-release on the largest accounts; no prerequisites.
  • Bring the electronic order channel into documented ownership opportunity — Gives the channel carrying 67.3 percent of orders a procedure and a named owner; no prerequisites and a prerequisite for later recovery work.

H2 — Recover failed orders once the channel is owned (6-18 months)

  • Catch and recover failed electronic orders before they become complaints opportunity — Targets 1196 unfulfilled electronic orders worth 12362493.74 EUR; scheduled after the channel has a documented owner and procedure.

H3 — Sustain and extend control (18+ months)

  • Embed continuous monitoring and periodic reconciliation as business-as-usual — Once credit, ownership, and recovery are live, sustain them through ongoing reconciliation and monitoring so divergence and unfulfilled orders do not re-accumulate.
