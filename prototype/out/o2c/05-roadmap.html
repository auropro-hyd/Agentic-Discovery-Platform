Transformation Roadmap

Sequenced across three horizons, aimed at a Control-first consolidation of order-to-cash before scaling automation direction.

H1 — Establish control and ownership foundations (0-6 months)

  • Single source of truth for credit standing opportunity — Resolves the €30,675,000 aggregate credit-limit difference across 267 accounts and restores policy-compliant approval; no prerequisites, highest business impact.
  • Formal ownership and procedure for the electronic channel opportunity — Assigns accountability for 67.3% of order volume and €59,711,399.53 of value currently run on informal notes; foundational for downstream recovery work.

H2 — Proactively protect electronic order revenue (6-18 months)

  • Automated monitoring and recovery of failed electronic orders opportunity — Targets the 1,196 unfulfilled electronic orders worth €12,362,493.74; sequenced after ownership is established so alerts route to a confirmed owner.

H3 — Sustain and extend controls (18+ months)

  • Embed continuous reconciliation and channel monitoring as standard practice — Consolidate the credit-data, ownership, and failure-recovery improvements into a durable operating routine with periodic review, building on the H1 and H2 foundations.
