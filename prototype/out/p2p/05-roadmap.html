Transformation Roadmap

Sequenced across three horizons, aimed at a Control-first procurement: enforce the policy at the point of commitment, then own and monitor it. direction.

H1 — Enforce approval on the largest orders (0-6 months)

  • Enforced second approval above the threshold opportunity — Protects the EUR 557,000 of single-approval spend, 91.9% of total value, and is the prerequisite for the later controls.

H2 — Close the order-first gap (6-18 months)

  • Order-first control before supplier commitment opportunity — Builds on enforced approval to remove the EUR 216,000 committed before an order existed, restoring budget control and matching.

H3 — Own and monitor the controls (18+ months)

  • Named owner and monitoring for procurement controls opportunity — Adds durable oversight over the enforced controls, surfacing concentrated non-compliance such as the EUR 149,000 in one buyer.
