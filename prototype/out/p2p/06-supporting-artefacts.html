Success metrics framework

How the impact of the recommended interventions should be measured once live — the dimension, what it means, and the target to hold delivery to.

MetricDefinitionTarget
Large commitments released under dual controlThe share of large purchase orders that release only after a second Finance approval is recorded, measured against today's seven orders and EUR 557,000 released on a single approval.Near-complete coverage — a material reduction in single-approval releases against the EUR 557,000 baseline, moving toward zero large orders released without a second approval.
Value protected by enforced second approvalThe value of large commitments held until dual control is satisfied, relative to the EUR 557,000 and 12.0 percent of total value currently bypassing the second approval.A sustained reduction in value released without a second approval against the EUR 557,000 baseline.
Orders placed with a purchase order firstThe share of orders confirmed to have a valid purchase order before being placed, measured against today's three maverick orders worth EUR 216,000.Near-complete compliance — a material reduction in orders placed outside the process against the EUR 216,000 baseline.
Recorded exceptions with an assigned ownerThe share of orders in a recorded exception state that have a named owner and a documented remediation outcome, across the two recorded approval states.Full ownership coverage — every recorded exception assigned to an owner, improving as the monitoring routine beds in.
Exceptions remediated within the review cycleThe share of surfaced exceptions driven to a recorded outcome within the agreed review window, relative to the EUR 557,000 and EUR 216,000 currently left unactioned.A rising share of recorded exceptions remediated each cycle, improving through tuning of the review routine.
