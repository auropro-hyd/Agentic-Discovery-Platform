1.   Purpose and Scope
This document defines the Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed (RACI) assignments for each
step of the Order-to-Cash process within Opella Europe. It provides a single reference for process ownership,
escalation authority, and inter-functional coordination requirements.
This RACI is the authoritative reference for O2C process accountability and must be read in conjunction with the
Order Management SOP (OPS-EU-CS-SOP-001) and the Credit Management Policy (OPS-EU-FIN-POL-002).
  RACI Key
R = Responsible: The role that performs the task or produces the output.
A = Accountable: The role that owns the outcome and approves the deliverable.
Only one A per process step.
C = Consulted: The role whose input is required before the step is completed.
I  = Informed: The role that must be notified when the step is completed or
when its outcome is known.

Operations; FD = Finance Director; VP = VP Commercial Ops.

This RACI covers Manual (telephone) and Email order channels only. EDI channel
process steps are not included in this version of the RACI. Formal accountability
assignments for EDI order processing will be documented upon completion of the
EDI SOP currently under development (target: Q3 2025). Personnel handling EDI
queries should refer to working notes OPS-EU-IT-WN-001 and contact the CS Team

4.   Governance and Review
4.1   RACI Maintenance
This RACI is owned by the VP Commercial Operations, Europe and must be reviewed annually or when there is
a material change to the O2C process, organisation structure, or systems landscape. Proposed changes must
be approved by the VP Commercial Operations and the Finance Director before taking effect.
4.2   Escalation Path
- For day-to-day process queries: CS Team Lead (A. Kovacs).
- For credit-related process queries: Credit Controller (R. Patel).
- For process ownership and governance: VP Commercial Operations (S. Marchetti).
4.3   EDI Process Documentation Status
The Order Management SOP (OPS-EU-CS-SOP-001, Section 1.3) notes that EDI order channel process
documentation is under development with a target publication date of Q3 2025. This RACI will be updated at
that time to include EDI-specific accountability assignments. Until that version is published, all EDI process
accountability questions must be directed to the CS Team Lead.
